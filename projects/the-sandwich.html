The Sandwich

//3D Animated Short

Summary The Sandwich is an animated short which describes the unfortunate fate of a few fresh ingredients. For this project, I was tasked with lighting, rigging, placing and animating objects. I also modelled and textured several assets, including the chef himself!
My Role Modeling, Character Rigging, Lighting, Texturing, Animating
Process Our animated short began with a series of simple sketches as we finalized our narrative. After, we collaborated together to model, texture, light, and animate our scene. After rendering each frame, we put on the final touches before presenting it to the class.
