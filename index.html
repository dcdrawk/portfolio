About Devin

//a brief intro all about this human

I'm a fourth year student at Simon Fraser University's School of Interactive Arts & Technology. I'm also an avid gamer, and I like to view a game as a set of tools needed to solve a problem of complete a quest. Likewise, SIAT has equipped me with a +5 Sword of Mastery needed to tackle any design project, ranging from web design, 3d animation, immersive game design, and much more.

My two co-op experiences gave me a +5 Shield of Fortitude when it comes to handling a multitude of design projects. These ranged designing posters, brochures, and flyers, to various web design CMS projects using Adobe CQ5 and Wordpress.

I pride myself on being able to solve problems. Whether it's sketching an interface or debugging my code, there's always something to learn and improve upon. Even if I stumble, I will find new ways to approach the design I am working on.
